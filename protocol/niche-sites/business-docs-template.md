## Notes on Generating Business Documents

1. **Order of creation:** Commercialization Strategy first → Business Plan (based on it) → Investors Pitch (distilled from both)
2. **Financial projections** should be conservative but credible. Base on the Lawra.org model as a baseline.
3. **Market data** should use real, current figures with cited sources where possible.
4. **Revenue projections** should show a realistic ramp: Year 1 is mostly platform-building with minimal revenue, Year 2 shows traction, Year 3 shows profitability.
5. **Expenses are standardized** across all sites (same team structure, same tools).
